Anything You Wanted To Know(lewisville)

A fun take on KERA's radio program "anything you wanted to know" but specific to LEWISVILLE.

A twitter meets reddit style forum catered to lewisville residents
wanting to ask questions specific to their city anonymously and get information in a condensed format.

This website was built using a Mongo database, Express and Node.js on the backend.
On the front-end the website was built using Angular2
